Questions? Answers.

Do I need a bank account or KYC?
Never. HoldPay works with email only. No bank account, no PAN card, no ID verification required on any plan.
What currencies can I accept?
On all plans you can accept USDT and USDC. Pro and Business plans support additional cryptocurrencies via OxaPay.
How does the transaction fee work?
The fee is deducted from each incoming payment. For example on the Pro plan, if you receive $100, you get $99.60. No monthly minimum, no setup fee.
Can I withdraw to UPI or PayPal?
Yes — on Pro and Business plans. Crypto withdrawal is available on all plans including Starter.
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?
Yes, you can change your plan anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately, downgrades at next billing cycle.
What is white label?
White label lets you run HoldPay under your own brand — your logo, your domain, your colors. Available on Business plan and Enterprise. Contact [email protected] for details.
